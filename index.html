Crystals Collector

How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score which varies for each crystal. The value of the crystals is hidden and changes with each game. If your number matches the "Match Number" you win, however if you go over you lose. Choose wisely!
